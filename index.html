ℹ️ How it works:

1. Encoding: Your text is converted to binary (8 bits per character), then split into 2-bit pairs. Each pair maps to a DNA base (A, T, C, or G) according to your custom mappings.

2. Visualization: The DNA sequence is displayed as colored blocks on the canvas.

3. Decoding: The process reverses - DNA bases convert to binary pairs, then to characters.

4. Re-simulate: Change the mappings anytime to see how different encoding schemes affect the DNA sequence!
